Privacy & Safety

How we protect users, why the physical Gram matters, and what data is (and isn’t) stored.

Why the physical Gram matters

A Gram of Art is a physical-first collectible. The digital experience exists to support the physical object — not replace it.

  • The physical Gram is required to claim ownership.
  • The physical Gram is required to redeem perks.
  • Screenshots or photos are not accepted.
Important: If you don’t have the physical Gram with you, perks cannot be redeemed.

Protection against misuse

The system is designed to reduce fraud and misuse through a mix of digital checks and human verification.

  • Each Gram can only be claimed once.
  • Perks can only be approved by partner staff.
  • Staff visually confirm the physical Gram before approval.
  • Cooldowns prevent rapid repeat redemptions.

What information is stored

Only the minimum information required to make the system work is stored.

  • Your customer account ID (to link Grams to you)
  • Which Grams you own
  • Perk redemption timestamps
Note: We do not store credit card details or payment information as part of the Gram system.

What is not stored

  • No location tracking
  • No background NFC scanning
  • No access to your phone beyond the tap interaction
  • No personal browsing data from your device

Your responsibility as an owner

Owning a Gram means taking care of the physical piece.

  • Keep your Gram safe.
  • Do not share photos as proof of ownership.
  • Bring the physical Gram when redeeming perks.
Important: Lost or damaged Grams may not be replaceable.

Questions or concerns

If you have questions about privacy or safety:

  • Review this Help Center.
  • Ask partner staff if you’re on-site.
  • Contact Soldac Studio support if needed.
