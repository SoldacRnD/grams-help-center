Redeeming Perks

How perks work at partner locations, what to do at the counter, and what “cooldown” means.

What a perk is

Some Grams include perks offered by partner businesses. A perk can be a free item, a discount, or a special benefit available to Gram owners.

Important: Perks are redeemed using the physical Gram. Screenshots or photos are not accepted.

Before you go to a partner

  • Bring the physical Gram.
  • Check your collection to see which partners offer perks.
  • Note any limits or cooldowns listed for the perk.
Tip: You don’t usually need your phone to redeem — the partner can validate by tapping the Gram.

At the counter (step-by-step)

  1. Tell staff you’d like to redeem a perk.
  2. Hand over the physical Gram.
  3. The staff member taps the Gram with their device.
  4. The validation screen confirms the perk.
  5. The staff approves the redemption.
Important: Staff will visually match the digital image with the physical Gram before approving.

Cooldowns & limits

Some perks can only be used once, or have a cooldown period before they can be redeemed again.

  • Single-use: The perk can be redeemed once.
  • Cooldown: The perk becomes available again after a set time.
Tip: If a perk is on cooldown, simply wait until the timer finishes before redeeming again.

Common questions

Do I need to log in at the counter?

No. In most cases the partner can validate by tapping the physical Gram.

Can I redeem a perk without the Gram?

No. The physical Gram is required to redeem perks.

The perk didn’t work — what now?

Ask staff to try tapping again. If it still fails, check Troubleshooting.
