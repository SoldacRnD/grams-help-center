Troubleshooting

Quick fixes for the most common issues when tapping, claiming, or redeeming a Gram.

The page is loading slowly

Sometimes the system needs a moment to wake up. This can happen on the first tap of the day.

  1. Wait up to 60 seconds.
  2. If nothing changes, refresh once.
  3. If needed, tap the physical Gram again.
Tip: A brief loading screen is normal. If it lasts more than a minute, try again once.

The tap didn’t open anything

  • Make sure NFC is enabled on your phone.
  • Tap the back of your phone directly on the Gram.
  • Remove thick cases or metal attachments.
  • Try tapping slowly and holding for 1–2 seconds.
Important: Some phones require NFC to be enabled in settings.

I don’t see the “Claim Piece” button

This can happen for a few reasons:

  • The Gram is already collected.
  • You’re not logged in.
  • The page didn’t fully load your login state.
  1. Confirm you are logged in.
  2. Refresh the page once.
  3. If needed, tap the Gram again.

I logged in but didn’t return to the Gram

After login, you may land on an account page instead of the Gram you were claiming. The system remembers your intent.

  1. Go back to the storefront home page.
  2. Look for a banner saying “Continue claiming your Gram”.
  3. Tap it to return to the correct Gram.
Tip: If you don’t see the banner, tapping the physical Gram again usually works.

A perk didn’t redeem

  • Make sure you brought the physical Gram.
  • Ask staff to tap the Gram again.
  • Check if the perk is on cooldown.
Important: Screenshots or photos cannot be used to redeem perks.

Still stuck?

If none of the steps above worked:

  1. Wait a few minutes and try again.
  2. Try a different phone if available.
  3. Contact the partner staff or reach out to support.
