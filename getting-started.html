Getting Started

What a Gram is, what happens when you tap, and how claiming works.

What A Gram of Art is

A Gram is a physical collectible artwork. Tapping it opens its digital page where you can view details, (sometimes) see partner perks, and claim ownership if it’s unclaimed.

What happens when you tap

  1. Tap the physical Gram with your phone (NFC enabled).
  2. You may see a short loading screen (the system is opening the correct page).
  3. You land on the Gram page with the artwork and details.
  4. If it’s unclaimed, you can claim it (login may be required).
Important: The physical Gram is the authority. Screenshots are not accepted for redemption.
